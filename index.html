Why HomeFuel

Every other app does one slice.
This is the whole loop.

Inventory apps don't cook. Meal planners don't know what's in your fridge. Calorie trackers make you type. HomeFuel connects all of it.

A kitchen that knows itself

Fridge, freezer, pantry — every item tracked with expiry urgency. After shopping, one tap puts the whole trip away.

The 30-second daily check

Swipe through what's expiring: still here, used up, trash it, buy more. Your inventory stays honest with zero typing.

Rescue meals

Use-first recipes built from exactly what's about to die in your fridge — from a 526-dish catalog tuned to your goal.

Weekly Systems

Whole-week meal plans — cut, maintain, or fuel a training block — filled in one tap and priced against your kitchen.

★ PLUS

Macros that log themselves

Cook a HomeFuel dish and calories, protein, carbs and fat land in your day automatically. No barcode, no search, no typing.

♛ PRO

Health, both directions

Workouts and weight sync with Apple Health; logged training estimates its burn and feeds your Move ring — no double counts.

How it works

Four habits. About two minutes a day.

1

Put your trip away

Come home from the store, open HomeFuel, and file the whole trip in one screen. Every item lands in its space — Fridge, Freezer, Pantry — with an expiry countdown already running. Shopped twice before putting away? Trips merge automatically.
